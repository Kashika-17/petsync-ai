# USER PERSONAS

**Project:** PetSync AI

**Version:** 1.0

---

# Persona 1 — Modern Pet Parent

Primary User

Age

22–45

Occupation

Any

Goals

- Stay organized
- Never miss pet care
- Store records safely
- Receive AI assistance

Pain Points

- Forgotten vaccinations
- Lost documents
- Multiple apps
- Poor organization

Needs

- Simplicity
- Automation
- AI
- Mobile Access

---

# Persona 2 — Multi-Pet Household

Households with multiple pets.

Goals

- Manage several pets easily
- Centralize information
- Share responsibilities

Pain Points

- Duplicate work
- Scattered records
- Reminder overload

Needs

- Pet Switcher
- Shared Dashboard
- Smart Reminders

---

# Persona 3 — Busy Professional

Works full time.

Goals

- Save time
- Receive proactive reminders
- Quick access to records

Pain Points

- Busy schedule
- Missed appointments
- Poor organization

Needs

- Automation
- AI Summaries
- Fast Navigation

---

# Persona 4 — Senior Pet Owner

Goals

- Keep aging pets healthy
- Track medication
- Monitor conditions

Pain Points

- Frequent vet visits

Needs

- Medication Tracking
