# Executive Summary

PetSync AI is an AI-powered pet management platform that helps pet parents organize, monitor, and improve their pet's health through intelligent automation.

The platform combines pet profile management, health records, vaccination tracking, medication reminders, AI-powered health summaries, document storage, and an intelligent AI assistant into a single web application.

Unlike traditional pet management apps, PetSync AI uses artificial intelligence to transform medical records into understandable insights, automate reminders, and provide personalized guidance for pet owners.

---

# Vision

To become the world's most trusted AI-powered operating system for pet parents.

---

# Mission

Empower every pet parent with intelligent tools that simplify pet care, improve health management, and reduce the stress of organizing important pet information.

---

# Problem Statement

Pet owners face several challenges:

- Medical records are scattered across different places.
- Vaccination schedules are forgotten.
- Medication reminders are missed.
- Health reports are difficult to understand.
- Pet information is not centralized.
- Emergency information is hard to access.
- Nutrition planning is confusing.
- There is no intelligent assistant dedicated to pet management.

---

# Proposed Solution

PetSync AI provides one intelligent platform where pet owners can:

- Manage multiple pets
- Store medical records
- Upload health reports
- Receive AI-generated summaries
- Track vaccinations
- Track medications
- Receive reminders
- Chat with an AI assistant
- Organize pet documents
- Access everything from one dashboard
